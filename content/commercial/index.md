Storsko Core is **Apache 2.0 open source** and fully self-hostable. For teams and enterprises that prefer managed hosting, the commercial platform at [app.storsko.com](https://app.storsko.com) adds:

- Managed hosting with automatic updates
- Multi-tenancy for agencies and consultancies
- Keycloak SSO integration
- Priority support and SLA guarantees
- Compliance reporting and audit exports

For pricing and plans, visit [storsko.com/pricing](https://storsko.com/pricing).

## Self-Hosting

The full commercial stack can also be self-hosted. See the [Deployment](../deployment/) guide for Docker-based setup instructions.
